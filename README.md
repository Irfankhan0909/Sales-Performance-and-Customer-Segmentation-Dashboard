Sales Performance & Customer Segmentation Dashboard (Superstore)

**Objective**  
Evaluate **sales, profit, customer segments, and regional performance** to support business growth strategies.

**Tools Used**  
Ms Excel(data handling), Power BI

**Key Work**
- Analyzed **sales, profit, region, category, and customer segment data**  
- Created **interactive dashboards** with filters and drill-downs  
- Identified **top and low performing regions and products**

**Business Value**  
Enables decision-makers to track **revenue growth, profitability, and customer trends** across locations and product categories.
